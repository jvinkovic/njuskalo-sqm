Privacy Policy for Njuškalo Price per m² Chrome Extension

This Chrome extension does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user information. It only reads publicly available data on the webpage (such as listing price and area) to calculate and display price per square meter.

No personal data is transmitted to any server or third party. The extension does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools.

Your privacy is fully respected while using this extension.
